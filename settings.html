Terms, Policies & Licenses

Terms of Service

By placing an order with Sumathi's Style, you agree to our custom-order process. All measurements, design preferences, and fabric details provided by you will be used exclusively to prepare your garment as specified. Please review your measurements and design instructions carefully before confirming, as orders are custom-made to your specifications. Once an order is confirmed and stitching work has commenced, changes to measurements, design, or fabric may not always be possible. We recommend reaching out to us via WhatsApp or phone before confirming if you have any doubts regarding sizing, style, or delivery timelines. By proceeding with your order, you acknowledge and accept these terms.

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled free of charge before stitching work has begun. Once stitching has started, the order enters production and materials/labour have already been committed, so custom-stitched items generally cannot be cancelled or refunded at that stage. If you wish to cancel an order, please contact us as soon as possible via WhatsApp or phone at 86107 03658 so we can check the status of your order and assist you accordingly. In case of any issue with the fit, quality, or delivery of your finished garment, please reach out to us within 3 days of delivery so we can review and resolve the matter fairly. We value your trust and always aim to work with you to find a suitable solution.

Privacy Policy

At Sumathi's Style, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Any details you share with us — including your name, phone number, email address, delivery address, measurements, and order preferences — are collected solely for the purpose of processing, stitching, and delivering your order. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing or any other unrelated purposes. Your data is stored securely and accessed only by our team for order-related communication and service. Payment information, if collected, is processed through secure and trusted payment gateways, and we do not store your card or banking details. By using our website and placing an order, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled, feel free to contact us at sumathisstyle@gmail.com or 86107 03658.

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sumathi's Style — Tailoring Boutique with 30 Years of Experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and delivery address.
  • Order Details: Measurements, design preferences, fabric choices, and order history.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (we do not store card details).
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device identifiers for security.
3. How We Use Your Data
  • To process and deliver your custom stitching orders.
  • To communicate order updates, delivery status, and promotional offers via SMS, WhatsApp, or Email (you can opt-out anytime).
  • To improve our services and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.
4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Trusted delivery partners for order fulfillment.
  • Payment gateways for secure transaction processing.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.
5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. We may also use cookies to enhance your browsing experience; you can disable cookies anytime through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights
  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of marketing communications anytime.
7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for use by individuals 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Order records are kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, contact our Grievance Officer at sumathisstyle@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 86107 03658.

De-activate my Account

Temporarily De-activate Account

De-activating your account will temporarily disable your access to Sumathi's Style. Don't worry — your orders, measurements, and preferences will remain safely saved. You can re-activate your account anytime, simply by logging back in.

What happens when you de-activate?
  • You won't be able to place new orders until you re-activate
  • Your saved wishlist, addresses, and measurements stay safe
  • Your Super Coins balance remains untouched
  • You'll stop receiving promotional messages and offers
  • Re-activating is easy — just log back in anytime
