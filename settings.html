Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sumathi's Style — Tailoring Boutique with 30 Years of Experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and delivery address.
  • Order Details: Measurements, design preferences, fabric choices, and order history.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (we do not store card details).
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device identifiers for security.
3. How We Use Your Data
  • To process and deliver your custom stitching orders.
  • To communicate order updates, delivery status, and promotional offers (with consent).
  • To improve our services and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.
4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Trusted delivery partners for order fulfillment.
  • Payment gateways for secure transaction processing.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.
5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights
  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of marketing communications anytime.
7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Order records are kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, contact our Grievance Officer at sumathisstyle@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 86107 03658.

De-activate my Account

Temporarily De-activate Account

De-activating your account will temporarily disable access. Your data, orders, and preferences will be preserved. You can re-activate anytime by simply logging back in.

What happens when you de-activate?
  • You won't be able to place new orders
  • Your wishlist and saved addresses remain saved
  • Your Super Coins balance is preserved
  • You won't receive marketing communications
